Student Registration

A key component of an enterprise resource planning (ERP) system, like Elite School Manager, is student registration. With the help of this tool, schools may manage and keep track of all the data pertaining to their students, including contact information, academic records, and extracurricular activities.

Schools may quickly add new students to the system, update existing student records, and have real-time access to student information with Elite School Manager's student registration tool. This can increase the precision and thoroughness of student records and streamline administrative procedures.

Fees Management

School fees management is a crucial feature of Elite School Manager, an ERP for school management. This feature allows administrators to easily create and manage fee schedules and payment plans, and allows parents to make payments online using a secure payment gateway. This streamlines the fee collection process and ensures that schools receive payments on time.

In addition to tracking and managing payments, Elite School Manager's school fees management module also provides valuable reporting and analysis capabilities. Administrators can generate reports to get a detailed view of fee collections and outstanding balances, and can use this information to make informed decisions about the school's financial health. This is particularly useful for schools that receive funding from multiple sources, such as government grants and private donations. Overall, the school fees management feature of Elite School Manager is an essential tool for any school that wants to improve its financial management and provide a better experience for students and their families.

Class Planner & Class Timetable

Class planner and class timetable are key features of Elite School Manager, an ERP for school management. The class planner allows administrators to easily create and manage class schedules, assigning teachers and students to specific classes and rooms. This ensures that classes are organized and run smoothly, and allows administrators to make changes to the schedule as needed.

Overall, the class planner and class timetable features of Elite School Manager are essential tools for any school that wants to improve its class management and provide a better experience for students and their families.

Result card generation is a valuable feature of Elite School Manager, an ERP for school management.
